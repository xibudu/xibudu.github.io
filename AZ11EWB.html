AZ11EWB -

11,000 BTU Portable Air Conditioner

 
 
BUY NOW
 
 

Accuzone Maxicool offers models with cooling levels and efficiencies to suit your needs from large master bedrooms or a large open space living room to small Room. The installation was hassle free and easy to maintain.

  • Powerful & Efficient Air Conditioner: Cool rooms of different sizes with Accuzone ASHRAE 11,000 BTU (6,000 BTU 2017 SACC Standard) portable air conditioner. Beat the heat and make you comfortable. It’s perfect for rooms with horizontal or vertical window installation of approx. 350 sq. ft. 

  • User-Friendly Programmable Control Function:Easy to operate. The Accozone portable air conditioner features an easy-to-read LED display on a compact remote control (batteries included) to set the time with a 24-hour timer, adjust the temperature between 61°F and 88°F, and change the working mode, from across the room.

  • Three-in-One Operation Model: The compact and modern floor air conditioner combines cooling, fan and dehumidifier models with a quiet performance for peaceful sleep, work and relaxing time.

  • Easy-to-Install and Easy-to-Move: All accessories are included in the package, with clear instructions and drawings to help you install quickly without the special tools. With wheels, the unit can easily be moved around in any room that is window accessible.

  • Washable Air Filter: The air conditioner has a removable and washable double-layer filter to protect you and your family from dust, pet dander and hair. We recommend that you clean the filter every two weeks of operation. Regular cleaning and maintenance will ensure its conditioning efficiency and help prevent air quality problems.

 
 

BEFORE FIRST USE

Allow the unit to sit UPRIGHT for at least 24 hours if your unit has NOT stored and transported in an UPRIGHT position.

 

PLEASE NOTE:

Turning AC unit OFF and ON again will trigger a 3-minute delay before cool air output resumes. This protects the internal compressor and is totally normal operation!
