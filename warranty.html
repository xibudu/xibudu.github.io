Warranty

Our three warranties protect your air conditioner unit. Register your air conditioner within 60 days of purchase to access our warranties.

30-Day Coverage for Initial Purchase

Defects in materials and workmanship of Covered Equipment that appear under normal use and maintenance are fully covered for 30 days and will include return shipping. This includes physical or working operation defects if the product is in such condition upon removal from the packaging at purchase time.

1-Year Limited Warranty Coverage

Accuzone Maxicool warrants the Covered Equipment to be free from defects in materials and workmanship, and will repair or replace, as its option, ANY PART of Covered Equipment installed in residential applications which fail in normal use and service within a 1-year warranty period.

3-year Compressor Warranty

Accuzone Maxicool warrants the Internal Compressor to be free from defects and workmanship, and will repair or replace, as its option, ANY PART of compressor in Covered Equipment installed in residential applications which fail in normal use and service within a 3-year warranty period.

For more information about our warranty policies, download our AC14MWS User Manual.
